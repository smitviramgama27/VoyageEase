Dubai

Information

Location:Dubai is one of the seven emirates that make up the United Arab Emirates (UAE). It is located on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, on the Persian Gulf.

Population:As of my last update, Dubai has a population of over 3 million people, with a significant expatriate community comprising people from various countries around the world.

Languages:The official language of Dubai is Arabic, but English is widely spoken and understood, especially in business and tourism sectors.

Culture:Dubai's culture is influenced by Islamic traditions, but it is also known for its cosmopolitan and diverse population. The city celebrates various cultural festivals and events throughout the year.

Economy:Dubai is a global city and a major hub for business, finance, trade, tourism, and aviation. It has a diversified economy with key sectors including real estate, construction, hospitality, and retail.

Tourist Attactions:Dubai is famous for its iconic skyscrapers, luxury shopping malls, stunning beaches, and world-class attractions. Key landmarks include the Burj Khalifa (the tallest building in the world), Palm Jumeirah (artificial island), Burj Al Arab (luxury hotel), Dubai Mall, and Dubai Fountain.
